Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our rental process.

How do I book equipment?
+
Fill in our contact form, call us directly, or browse the online catalog to select the equipment you need. We confirm availability within 24 hours.
Is maintenance included in the rental?
+
Yes — all rentals include a full pre-delivery service. Professional plan customers also receive priority on-call maintenance throughout their hire.
Do you deliver to farms?
+
Absolutely. We deliver and collect across all 14 parishes in Jamaica. Delivery is included in all weekly and seasonal hire packages.
What is the minimum rental period?
+
Our minimum hire period is 1 day. Weekly and monthly packages offer better value for longer operations.
Can I extend a rental mid-hire?
+
Yes — contact our team at least 24 hours before your rental end date and we will arrange an extension subject to availability.
