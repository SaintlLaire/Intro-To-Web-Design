Our Story

From a small depot to Jamaica's most trusted equipment rental service.

AgriRent is a farm supply store located in Nain, St. Elizabeth, established in 2010 by a father-and-son duo, Mr. Clyde Jones and Mr. Clyde Jones Jr. The business was founded in response to the challenges they faced in sourcing essential farming supplies, which required traveling to two neighboring parishes. Through their own experiences and discussions with other farmers, they identified a widespread issue; many farmers struggled to access necessary tools and equipment due to high costs and limited availability.

Recognizing this gap in the market, AgriRent was born, introducing affordable buying options and rental services as a cost-effective alternative to purchasing. This innovative approach has since become a cornerstone of business.

AgriRent is committed to providing affordable and reliable farming solutions through competitive pricing and flexible rental options. The rental service has proven highly successful, enabling farmers to access essential equipment without the financial burden of ownership. This support has helped many farmers sustain and expand their operations while continuing to provide for their families.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AgriRent and how we work.

How long has AgriRent been operating?
+
AgriRent was founded in 2010 and has been serving Jamaican farmers for over 15 years. We started with 5 tractors in St. Catherine and now operate island-wide with a fleet of 500+ machines.
Which parishes do you operate in?
+
We deliver and collect across all 14 parishes in Jamaica. Delivery is included in all weekly and seasonal hire packages at no extra charge.
Do you offer training on equipment use?
+
Yes. All Professional plan customers receive a complimentary equipment orientation session from one of our trained technicians before first use.
Is maintenance included in the rental?
+
All rentals include a full pre-delivery service check. Professional plan customers also receive priority on-call maintenance throughout their hire period.
