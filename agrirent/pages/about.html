Our Story

From a small depot to Jamaica's most trusted equipment rental service.

AgriRent was founded in 2010 by a team of agricultural engineers who saw a gap in the Jamaican market. Small and mid-sized farms had no affordable way to access modern machinery without making enormous capital investments.

Starting with a fleet of five tractors in St. Catherine, we now operate island-wide, maintaining over 500 machines and supporting more than 2,000 farmers across all 14 parishes.

Our mission has never changed: to make professional-grade agricultural equipment accessible, affordable, and always ready when you need it.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about AgriRent and how we work.

How long has AgriRent been operating?
+
AgriRent was founded in 2010 and has been serving Jamaican farmers for over 15 years. We started with 5 tractors in St. Catherine and now operate island-wide with a fleet of 500+ machines.
Which parishes do you operate in?
+
We deliver and collect across all 14 parishes in Jamaica. Delivery is included in all weekly and seasonal hire packages at no extra charge.
Do you offer training on equipment use?
+
Yes. All Professional plan customers receive a complimentary equipment orientation session from one of our trained technicians before first use.
Is maintenance included in the rental?
+
All rentals include a full pre-delivery service check. Professional plan customers also receive priority on-call maintenance throughout their hire period.
